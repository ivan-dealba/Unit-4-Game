Crystal Collector

A number will be randomly generated for you at the start of the game.

Below there are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

If you match your total score to the random number, you win. If your total score exceeds the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The values of each crystal and the random number will be changed at the start of each new game.
